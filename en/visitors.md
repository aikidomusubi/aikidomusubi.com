# Visitors

We welcome aikido practitioners from around the world who are visiting Barcelona and wish to train with us. Whether you are traveling for a short stay or considering joining our dojo, we are happy to receive you. ## Training as a Visitor

Everybody is welcome.

If you wish to participate in training, please contact us in advance. If you wish to visit with a group, please contact us at least __two weeks in advance__.

Before contacting us, please check our [training schedule]({{ site.url }}/{{ page.lang }}/training-schedule/) to see which classes are available during your stay.

When contacting us, please include the following information:

- Your full name
- The name of your dojo
- Your grade
- The name of your instructor
- The date(s) and class(es) you wish to participate in

This helps us prepare and ensure a smooth experience for everyone on the mat.

## If You Wish to Observe a Class

- Please let us know in advance the day and class you wish to observe.
- Please arrive at least __15 minutes before__ the class starts.

Observation is a good option if you are new to the dojo, visiting for a short time, or simply curious about our practice.

## If You Wish to Become a Member of Aikido Musubi

__Beginners__<br>
If you are new to aikido, we recommend attending a __beginners class__ before formally applying to join the dojo.

Beginners classes focus on basic movements, etiquette, and safety, and are designed to help you become familiar with aikido practice in a supportive environment. No prior experience is required.

Once you have practiced one class, please notify us of the day you wish to start training. You may do this __in person at the dojo__ or __by email__.

We recommend reviewing our [fees and membership information]({{ site.url }}/{{ page.lang }}/fees/) before joining. __Registration is free of charge__. We offer discounts for families. And we also give you 2 classes free trial with no obligation

__Aikido Practitioners from Other Dojos__<br>
If you are already practicing aikido at another dojo and wish to join __Aikido Musubi__, please let us know:

- Your full name
- The name of your dojo
- Your grade
- The name of your instructor
- The date and classes you wish to start training

You may also choose to __observe a class__ or __practice as a visitor__ before joining. Please refer to our [fees and membership information]({{ site.url }}/{{ page.lang }}/fees/) for details. There is __no registration fee__.
